Burrow Blasters

Unity · C# · 2D Platformer · Mar 2026 · Team assignment

The brief

An anonymous team handed us a GDD for an 80s arcade game without telling us what the source material was. Our job was to interpret it and build it. We figured out fairly early it was Donkey Kong — the vertical structure, the barrel logic, the ladder climbing all gave it away. We built a vertical-scrolling platformer from that interpretation.

What I built

I was responsible for all UI — main menu, game over screen, and the in-game HUD including health display, score counter, and lives. Making the HUD feel readable during fast gameplay without cluttering the screen was the main design challenge.

What I learned

Reading a GDD critically and making judgement calls where it's ambiguous. The document didn't specify everything, so we had to decide what "felt right" based on the source game's design intent rather than just following instructions literally.
